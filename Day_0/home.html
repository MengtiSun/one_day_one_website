About This Site

I saw the 180 websites in 180 days built by Jennifer Dewalt and come up with the idea to implement her websites with my own code.

Although my websites look similar to hers, I use my own method to get the same layouts and animation effects.

Each website is small but interesting. They cover almost all the essential knowledge used in HTML, CSS, Javascript and many other latest front-end language standards. I enjoy every moment of making these websites and hope you also like them.
